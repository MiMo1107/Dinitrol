Information about Dinitrol

Dinitrol has more than 70 years of experience working with corrosion-protection, worldwide.

All of Dinitrols Rust protection centers are subjected to Denmark's only approved control the "VKO-R" under the Danish Techological Institute.

The Danish winter is extra hard on your car. to ensure the road holding in the cold months our roads are salted, salt combined with moisture aggravates corrosion by 25%.
